Different types of lists in html

Different types of lists in html are -
i)UnOrdered list
ii)Ordered list

Unordered list

  • Item 1
  • item 2
  • item 3
    • sub item 1
    • sub item 2
    • sub item 3
  • item 4

Ordered list

  1. Item 1
  2. item 2
    1. sub item 1
    2. sub item 2
  3. item 3
    • sub item 1
    • sub item 2
    • sub item 3
  4. item 4
